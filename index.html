After earning a degree in Computer Engineering and building mobile apps for large user bases at Anadolu Insurance Company, I noticed that technically strong products often failed to scale because they lacked strategic marketing and consumer insight. I pursued an MSc in Marketing at Queen Mary University of London to bridge this gap, aligning technology with customer behaviour, storytelling, and data-driven growth.

I bring a partner mindset to cross-functional teams by combining analytical rigour, stakeholder communication, and execution discipline to support growth, efficiency, and customer engagement across enterprise organisations.
